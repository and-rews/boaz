Reservation Policy: Reservations must be made in advance, and confirmed with a deposit. Deposits must be received within 48 hours of making the reservation, or the reservation may be canceled. Full payment is due 30 days prior to the arrival date. Payment can be made via credit card, PayPal, or bank transfer.

Cancellation Policy: If the reservation is canceled 30 days or more prior to the arrival date, a full refund of the deposit will be issued. If the reservation is canceled within 30 days of the arrival date, no refund will be issued. No refunds will be issued for early departures.

Check-In and Check-Out Policy: Check-in time is 4:00 pm or later on the day of arrival. Check-out time is 11:00 am on the day of departure. Late check-outs may be available upon request, subject to availability and an additional fee.

Occupancy Policy: The lodge has a maximum occupancy of [insert number] guests. Any additional guests beyond the maximum occupancy must be approved in advance and may be subject to an additional fee.

Pet Policy: Pets are allowed, subject to approval and an additional fee. Guests are responsible for any damages or cleaning required as a result of their pets.

Smoking Policy: Smoking is strictly prohibited in the lodge and on the surrounding property. Guests who violate this policy may be subject to an additional cleaning fee and/or forfeiture of their security deposit.

Damage Policy: Guests are responsible for any damages caused to the lodge or its contents during their stay. The lodge reserves the right to charge guests for any damages caused, and to withhold any portion of the security deposit to cover the cost of repairs or replacements.

Housekeeping Policy: The lodge will be cleaned before each guest's arrival. Daily housekeeping is not provided, but can be arranged for an additional fee. Guests are responsible for cleaning up after themselves during their stay and leaving the lodge in a tidy condition upon departure.
